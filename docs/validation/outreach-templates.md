# Outreach Templates

Use these to book Milestone 1 calls.

Keep messages short. The ask is a research call, not a demo.

## Founder DM

Subject: Quick question on stablecoin payments

Hi {{name}},

I am researching how teams that already receive USDC/USDT handle the messy part after payment: matching wallet activity to customers, invoices, receipts, support cases, and reconciliation records.

If your team accepts stablecoins, I would love to learn how you handle this today. Not selling anything. I am trying to validate whether this pain is real enough to build around.

Open to a 20-minute call this week?

## Developer DM

Subject: How are you matching stablecoin payments?

Hi {{name}},

I am talking with teams that accept stablecoins and want to understand the operational layer around payment detection, matching, receipts, webhooks, and exceptions.

The specific question: when a transaction lands in a wallet, how do you connect it to the right customer, invoice, or order?

Would you be open to sharing how your team handles this today?
